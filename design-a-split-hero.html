How we help

Free, impartial guidance

We walk through your income, outgoings and debts with you, then explain every realistic option - not just one product - so you can make an informed decision.

IVAs for sole traders

An IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) can let you make one reduced monthly payment over a fixed period, with remaining qualifying debt written off at the end - while you continue to trade. We'll tell you honestly whether it's right for you, or whether another route is better.

Other paths we may discuss

Time to Pay arrangements with HMRC, informal negotiations with creditors, or - if it's genuinely in your best interests - restructuring or winding the business up in an orderly way.
