How we help

1
Free, impartial guidance

We walk through your income, outgoings and debts, then explain every realistic option - not just one product - so you can make an informed decision.

2
IVAs for sole traders

An IVA can let you make one reduced monthly payment over a fixed period, with remaining qualifying debt written off at the end - while you keep trading. We'll tell you honestly whether it's right for you.

3
Other paths we may discuss

Time to Pay arrangements with HMRC, informal negotiations with creditors, or - only if it's genuinely in your best interests - an orderly wind-down.
